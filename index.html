    • My experience working as a personal trainer

      I’ve always been interested in fitness and health, but i always struggled with keeping a healthy weight and it wasn’t until i got to my biggest weight of 320 pounds that realized i had to knuckle down and learn how to take control and i did and I started working as a personal trainer and thats when I realized how much I loved helping others reach their fitness goals. Seeing the progress my clients made – both in terms of their physical appearance and their overall health – was incredibly rewarding, and I knew I wanted to make a career out of it. Now, as a certified personal trainer, I get to help people every day change their lives for the better. Whether it’s helping someone lose weight, gain muscle, or just get in better shape overall, I take great pride in knowing that I’m making a difference in someone’s life. If you’re thinking about becoming a personal trainer, I highly recommend it – it’s one of the most gratifying careers you can have. I got into personal training because I wanted to help people achieve their fitness goals. I love seeing the progress my clients make and hearing their success stories. Working as a personal trainer is very rewarding, but it can also be challenging at times. It requires a lot of knowledge and understanding of human anatomy and physiology, as well as exercise science. You need to be able to design safe and effective workout programs that meet your clients' individual needs. It's also important to be able to motivate and encourage your clients, so they stay on track with their goals. There will be days when they don't feel like working out, and it's your job to help them push through those tough times. Overall, I absolutely love being a personal trainer. It's an incredibly fulfilling career that I'm grateful to have.
